# Code of conduct

ModelSunset contributors are expected to keep project spaces respectful, constructive, and safe.

- Discuss ideas and code, not personal characteristics.
- Welcome questions and good-faith corrections.
- Do not harass, threaten, discriminate, publish private information, or disrupt collaboration.
- Maintainers may edit or remove unacceptable contributions and restrict participation when necessary.

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through GitHub. Reports will be reviewed discreetly and in good faith.
